Be Bold, Be LOKO.

LOKO is a Cornell University-based dance crew founded in 2015 that is aimed at promoting Korean music, fine arts, and culture through dance performances and videos. As a contemporary dance group, we specialize in but are not limited to, K-pop and K-hip hop dances. We explore not only the realm of Korean culture but also the intertwining of global music genres and Korean music. LOKO has a special commitment to promoting awareness of Korean music not only to the Cornell community through performances on campus but also to the public through official choreography videos online.
